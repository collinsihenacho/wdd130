Dry Oar Rafting

Anyone can go for whitewater rafting, including children from age 5 above and those with no serious health condition including pregnant woman. As long as you are physically fit and up for the adventure, age is not a barrier.

Company information

Our vision is to create a sustainable and equitable reality where our rivers are enjoyed and protected, a place where families and friends can be inspired and challenged. Dry Oar Whitewater Tours has been running rivers since we were first founded in 1978. Since we opened 40+ years ago we have expanded to include Adventure Connection, Mariah Wilderness Expeditions, High Desert River Outfitters, Canyon Outfitters and are the parent company of Abuja. While some things have changed over the years, Dry Oar remains a family-run small business with the same passion for the river and the missionof leading others to experience it.
